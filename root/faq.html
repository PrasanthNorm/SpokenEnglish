Frequently Asked Questions

What makes your English course unique?

Our course uniquely combines English language learning with Bible passages, providing familiar context for Telugu speakers. We focus on practical communication skills while using the Bible's rich language as a learning tool.

Is this a religious course?

No, this is not a religious course. While we use the Bible as a teaching resource due to its clear language and familiar stories, our focus is purely on English language learning. The course is open to students of all faiths.

What are the class timings?

We offer two flexible time slots to accommodate different schedules:

  • Morning Batch: 7:00 AM
  • Evening Batch: 8:00 PM

How much does the course cost?

We offer two pricing options:

  • Individual Classes: ₹700/- per month
  • Pair Classes: ₹1000/- per month
Fee concessions are available for students from economically disadvantaged backgrounds.

How does the money-back guarantee work?

We offer a 100% money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with the course. This reflects our confidence in our teaching methodology and commitment to your learning success.

What is the duration of the course?

The course duration is flexible and depends on your learning goals and starting level. We recommend a minimum of 3 months for noticeable improvement in your English speaking skills.

Do you provide study materials?

Yes, we provide all necessary learning materials, including carefully selected Bible passages, practice exercises, and supplementary resources to support your learning journey.
